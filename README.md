# Simple Interest Calculator

A simple script/tool that calculates simple interest based on the principal amount, annual interest rate, and time period in years.

## Formula
$$\text{Simple Interest} = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100}$$

Where:
- **P** = Principal amount
- **R** = Annual interest rate (in %)
- **T** = Time period (in years)

## Usage
1. Enter the principal amount.
2. Enter the rate of interest per annum.
3. Enter the time duration in years.
4. Calculate the total interest and final balance.
